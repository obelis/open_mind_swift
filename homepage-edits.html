About us

When it comes to hearing improvement, Swift Audiology’s Debra Swift admits that deciding where to get hearing aids can be very confusing, with a wide array of choices in providers, brands, price ranges and styles. Debra Swift has over 25 years of experience that allows her to help her patients cut through the confusion to obtain a personalized solution that is convenient, customized and affordable. When a person is confused and afraid of making the wrong choice, it often leads them away from seeking help. The questions are; “Am I choosing the right professional? Am I going to spend money on a hearing aid that may not help me? Will my choice end up in a drawer?” Swift Audiology removes those fears with no obligation trials, liberal return periods, experienced and skilled Professionals who provide excellence in follow up care. Swift Audiology provides rehabilitative counseling, verification testing and sets good practices, both at home and during our office visits after the fitting, in order for the Patient to experience a lifetime of Better Hearing.
